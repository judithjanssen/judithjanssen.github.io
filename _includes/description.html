What to expect?

Attention, in all its varieties, has a very powerful influence on the way we feel and the way we move through life.

The internal focus helps us connect with our intuitions and values that guide us, favoring the process of decision making; the external focus helps us navigate the world around us; and focus on others improves our social life.

Modern society however, does not facilitate the cultivation of attention.

The speeded-up, highly complex society we live in, where we have a non-stop stream of distractions coming in through our smartphones and laptops feeds our so-called monkey mind, with very short attention spans and scattered thoughts.

The good news is: you can train your brain! (and your body while you are at it :)


I invite you to step into the miraculous world that is your body and mind, become close, and start refining your inner focus.
