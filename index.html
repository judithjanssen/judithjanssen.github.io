About me

I am a Dutch training specialist currently based in the beautiful Marina Alta (Alicante, Spain).
I host and organize workshops, lessons and retreats where I focus on body awareness, proper breathing, postural alignment and mindfulness. My teaching is an integration of Hatha yoga with the precision and props of Iyengar yoga, the dynamics of Vinyasa flow and other body movement and expression techniques.
